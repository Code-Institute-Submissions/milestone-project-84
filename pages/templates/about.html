About Us

BarTender is Galways premier cocktail bar. BarTender brings together the a classic cocktail club atmosphere and the best mixologist to produce an unparallelled experience

With the current COVID-19 pandemic BarTender has lauched this website as a way to book a seat in our bar and provide a completely contactless experience.

Please use this site to make a booking request so that we can allocate you a seat for you or your group. Once at the bar you can then use this site to look through our menu, choose your drinks and then order and pay.

This allows you to limit the surfaces you are in contact with as well as the number of our staff members you have to be in contact with.

We hope you enjoy using the BarTender website. If yuou have an suggestions on how to improve the site please let us know through our contact page.
